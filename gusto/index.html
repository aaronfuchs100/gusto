In the heart of Gardens, GUSTO Cafeteria welcomes each day with freshly ground organic coffee, 100% whole-food juices and a wide selection of breakfast options.

Those in a hurry can grab a croissant or freshly baked muffin, whilst a full range of egg breakfasts awaits those with time to spare.

Our lunchtime buffet showcases local, seasonal fare suitable for vegetarian and carnivore alike, whilst our sandwich selection includes the ever popular Chicken-Chilli-Mayo, Franschoek Smoked Salmon, or Aubergine Pesto.

By night our humble courtyard is transformed into a fine dining table of 20 suitable for any celebration.
